About Our Project

Optimizing Your Travel Experience

Pathfinder helps travelers explore tourist spots efficiently by finding the best routes between attractions. Our application uses Dijkstra's Algorithm to calculate the optimal path from your current location to various points of interest in any district.

Did you know? Our route optimization can save travelers up to 40% of their travel time between attractions!

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, our platform provides personalized recommendations based on your preferences, time constraints, and transportation options.

With an engaging and interactive user interface, real-time updates, and detailed information about each attraction, Pathfinder ensures that you make the most of your travel experience.

Important Details

Route Optimization

Using Dijkstra's Algorithm, our application calculates the shortest path between attractions, helping you minimize travel time and maximize your sightseeing experience.

Interactive UI

Enjoy a user-friendly interface with interactive maps, smooth animations, and intuitive controls that make planning your itinerary a breeze.

Real-time Updates

Stay informed with real-time updates on attraction wait times, weather conditions, and other important factors that might affect your travel plans.
